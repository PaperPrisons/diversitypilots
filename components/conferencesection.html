Conference Program

Innovator Diversity Pilots Conference

September 20, 2024

Atlanta, Georgia • Emory University School of Law

About the Conference

Join us for a groundbreaking conference bringing together researchers, practitioners, and industry leaders to advance diversity and inclusion in innovation. Featuring keynote presentations, research insights, and practical strategies from leading experts in the field.
